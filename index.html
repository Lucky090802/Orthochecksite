About OrthoCheck

Overview

OrthoCheck is a digital platform designed to streamline musculoskeletal (MSK) assessments, replacing traditional in-person visits to healthcare providers. Through an interactive process that includes both interview and physical assessments, OrthoCheck provides individuals with quick, personalized insights into their MSK health, all from the comfort of their home.

Purpose

The primary purpose of OrthoCheck is to enhance accessibility and convenience for individuals needing MSK assessments, offering a reliable, AI-driven tool that helps assess symptoms, suggest preliminary recommendations, and provide feedback on the potential next steps for care, reducing the need for time-consuming hospital visits.

Problem It Solves

  • Access to Healthcare: OrthoCheck allows individuals to access MSK health assessments without the need for an in-person consultation.
  • Time Efficiency: It saves time by automating the assessment process and offering immediate insights and recommendations.
  • Cost Reduction: By offering a virtual alternative to traditional MSK assessments, it reduces costs associated with hospital visits.
  • Accessibility for Remote Areas: Those living in remote areas without easy access to healthcare facilities can benefit from OrthoCheck's services.

Features

  • Interactive Assessments: The platform includes both interview and physical assessment features, providing a comprehensive evaluation of the user's MSK health.
  • AI Recommendations: Based on the results of the assessments, OrthoCheck offers personalized recommendations for the next steps in treatment or care.
  • Emergency Alerts: In case of urgent or severe symptoms, an emergency flag notifies the user of the need to seek immediate medical attention.
  • Offline Functionality: The AI-driven symptom analyzer can work offline, making the platform more accessible in areas with limited internet access.
